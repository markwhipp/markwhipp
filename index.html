Project Management / Strategy And Planning

  • Work with clients and departments to problem solve and find IT solutions and workflows.
  • Plan and specify IT requirements and, where required, liaise with third party service providers.
  • Project manage implementation of new systems and procedures.
  • Procurement of Internet Service Provider services and other Cloud based hosted services.
  • Setup and management of Google Workspace and collaboration tools.
  • Preparation of backup procedures to safeguard key data from hardware system failures.
  • Preparation and review of data security procedures.
  • Planning of system upgrades, repairs and hardware replacements with minimal impact on system uptime.
  • Finding software and hardware solutions that maximise performance but keep costs in line with SME IT budgets.
  • Handle new site openings from planning stage, service provider orders to installation and configuration of network devices and WiFi systems.
